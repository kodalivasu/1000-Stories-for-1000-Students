1,000 Stories for 1,000 Students
A Story-Powered Global Learning & Impact Platform
🌟 VISION

To build a global community where storytelling fuels education, creativity bridges cultures, and 1,000 children gain access to sustained learning opportunities over 10 years.

We envision a world where:

Children don’t just receive education — they participate in shaping it.

Creativity becomes a tool for social impact.

Students across different economic backgrounds collaborate, learn, and grow together.

Young leaders prove that stories can change lives.

🎯 MISSION

To support 100 underprivileged children per year for the next 10 years — through a storytelling-driven platform that:

Publishes and shares original children’s books.

Builds a community of donors and creators.

Transparently tracks educational impact.

Provides supplemental learning tools for supported students.

Connects classrooms across geographies.

💡 WHAT THIS PLATFORM IS

A storytelling studio.

A youth-led global education community.

🌟 Community members & donors
