## StoryNest — Kids E-Book App

**Source:** [Kids E-Book App – Playful Storytelling UI Design](https://dribbble.com/shots/26797109-Kids-E-Book-App-Playful-Storytelling-UI-Design) (Dribbble)

**Why we like it:** Soft pastels, clear hierarchy, large touch targets, playful but not busy. Covers home/discovery, book detail with audio player, and reading view with Audio/Text toggle and tap-to-pronounce (highlighted words). Good fit for our kid audience and reader requirements (R1–R6).

**Screens to adapt for web:** Recommendations carousel, Categories, Book detail + audio controls, Reading view with font size and word tap.
